I utilized inferences from my analysis to base developmental decions I was to make. This sparked my interest in the field of Data Science.

I thouroughly enjoyed my time compiling all my research and abstracting it down into an easily digestible and marketable presentation to present to interested companies. These presentations were well recieved by multiple market-leading cargo carrier companies.
