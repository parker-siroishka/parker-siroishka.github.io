Catholic Family Services

Youth Mentor

Fall 2016 to Present

As a member of a varsity sport at the University of Calgary I was given the opportunity to volunteer for Catholic Family Services. This organization pairs a varsity athlete of a Calgary university with an at-risk youth through the Boys and Girls Club of Calgary. A weekly meetup is arranged and you get together with your mentee to play games, hangout, and just talk about life.

Being a part of this program has been beyond rewarding as I have seen shy, timid kids change into leaders and role models for the other young kids in the program. I've developed much stronger communication skills through this proccess and look to continue to grow as a strong and responsible role model for kids to strive towards.
